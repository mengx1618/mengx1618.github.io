Who Am I

My name is Xue Meng, the creater of this website.

I am originally from a small city in China. 8 years before, I came to the United States for college. Minneapolis MN was my first stop, and I went to University of Minnesota there. My major was Statistics. After finshing my undergraduate career, I went to Miami FL for my graguate study and spent one year there. I learned business analytics in the University of Miami. I have to say that Miami is one of the most fun city I've ever been. I made a lot of good friends there. We hanged out a lot.

My Work

After graduating, I got an offer from MicroSoft Corporation. That was the reason why I ended up in Seattle WA. I worked as a Data Analyst there. It was a very valuable experience in my whole life, not only because it was my first job, but also because I got the opportunity to meet so many cool people and learn so much stuff in my career. Unfortunately, after 2 years' working in MicroSoft, I quitted for some personal issue. Right now I am a full-time student in Bellevue College, and I am thrive on the study of programming language. Though life in school is quite different from that at Microsoft, it is full of fun too. And there are also quite a lot stuff to learn.

My life

The bigger one is Lilly. She is a lovely girl and she is one year and a half now. She is like her mama, from Minneapolis as well. The little one names Archie, he is half year old.
